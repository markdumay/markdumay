### Hi there! I'm Mark Dumay

Mark Dumay is co-founder of [adaptiQ](https://weareadaptiq.nl), a digital transformation boutique based in the Netherlands. With more than 20 years of experience, Mark has helped numerous clients in Utilities, Telecom, and Wholesale to get more value from data and analytics.

Mark holds a Master in Computer Science from Delft University of Technology and is a certified Lean Six Sigma Black Belt. He is the maintainer of [Hinode](https://gethinode.com), a theme for [Hugo](https://gohugo.io).
